Service Providers

Plastic payment cards — credit and debit cards — are the most common form of payment all over the world, next to cash. Toomi offers a payment product platform used by mobile money service providers to develop credit and debit card programs for their customers.

Become a card issuer, a merchant acquirer, and make everyday transactions more convenient, reliable and secure for individual and business customers.

Governments

Toomi is working with governments and public institutions to change the way people pay and are paid. Adopting electronic payments can create significant cost savings and efficiencies, minimize reliance on cash and the corruption that dealing in cash creates.

Our public sector solutions address a variety of payment flows that matter most to government. These include tools for enhanced procurement and payment processes, management of travel related expenses, general spend management and reporting.
