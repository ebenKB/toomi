Getting Started

Overview

Toomi links mobile money services together with a global platform that enables providers to issue debit and credit cards to consumers. Cards are linked to mobile wallets and work like Visa or Mastercard.

Obtain your card

Visit the office of your mobile money service provider and apply for a debit or credit card.

Discover the world

A Toomi-branded card provides the opportunity to express yourself - your way, anytime, anywhere.

Make payments

Pay at the counter

Swipe your card in a point-of-sale terminal at the grocery shop or at the hair salon.

Order stuff online

Place orders online and pay for them with your Toomi-branded card, just like Visa and Mastercard.

Settle bills

Enter your card details in your preferred mobile payment app, then settle bills and services.

Travel with Toomi

Use your card anywhere

Pay at partner shops and restaurants, anywhere in the world against your mobile money account at home.

Withdraw cash

Take your card with you abroad and withdraw cash from partner ATMs, in any currency.

Send money across providers

Transfer funds to mobile money users on other networks, including those in other countries.

Toomi Payment Platform

Extending financial services and economic opportunities to everyone.

Partners

We are working with mobile financial service providers and payment processors to extend the reach of mobile wallet services and make them accessible to everyone.

Cardholders

Toomi does not issue cards or extend credit to consumers. Obtain a debit or credit card from your mobile money service provider.

Merchants

To serve your customers better by accepting Toomi card payments, contact your mobile money service provider for their merchant services.

Fees and charges

For details on card fees and transaction charges, contact your mobile money service provider or payment processor.
