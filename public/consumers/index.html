Choose your card

There’s the right card for everyone. Apply for a Toomi credit, debit, or prepaid card with your mobile money provider. Credit cards come handy when you have to make that unexpected payment. Debit cards give you access to to the money in your mobile wallet with no interest to pay. And if you don’t have a mobile money account, you can load money onto a Toomi prepaid card and use it for shopping and payments.

Spend freely

Make everyday purchases in-store, online, or in-app quickly and safely with your Toomi card. It’ll be difficult to run out of possibilities with your card. Make a long trip to visit relatives, send money to a friend in another country, settle utility bills, top up fuel in your car, pay for your new hair-do at the salon.
